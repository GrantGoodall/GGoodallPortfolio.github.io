Boston Scientific R&D

Tesla Internship

Mechanical Engineering Internship in Tesla's Battery Department. Worked on creating, testing, and supplying Cybertruck parts. Water Ingress Testing, Supply Chain Negotiations, and Part Design.

Draper Knitting Collaboration

Worked with Draper Knitting to find out why certain yarn was breaking and how to decrease the breakage. The process included interviewing employees and management, materials testing on the yarn, reverse engineering the machine components, and prototyping/proposing several scalable paths forwards with implementation plans.

Medical Supply Drone Airdrop

Modified a drone with a launch, airdrop, and recovery system. The modified drone is designed to parachute medical supplies for easier delivery to remote Canadian islands.

Machined Aluminum Box

I used CAD and CAM to mill this aluminum box. The dowel and diamond pins use interference and slip fits to hold a tight tolerance while opening the box.

Resin Cast Nightlight

I made this nightlight through 3D printing, silicon molding, urethane casting, plastic thermoforming, and soldering.< The silicone mold and thermoforming mold were designed so that assembly uses minimum adhesives. /p>

Injection Molded Pin

I made these pins through using CAD and CAM to machine an aluminum mold and then overmolded and trimmed the pins.

Neurotech EMG / Neural Net Rock Paper Scissors

Used Electromyography (EMG) sensors to train a neural net that can identify "Rock", "Paper", and "Scissors" gestures in real time.

Crazyflie Swarm Robotics

Used Local Repair A* to simulate "pick and place" warehouse behavior for a multi-agent crazyflie swarm.

Quadcopter Control Algorithm

Used angular momentum principles to design and tune a PID controls algorithm for a linearized quadcopter model.
